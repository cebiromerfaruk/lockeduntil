Use Cases

Investment Discipline

Lock your own instructions to the future to limit emotional decisions and prevent panic selling.

Sample Flow: An investment plan is saved and the unlock date set after a volatile period.

Ethical Note: Content is not financial advice; financial decisions are your responsibility.

Will / Last Wish

Securely stores instructions or messages that must be executed after death.

Sample Flow: The user saves the will text, sets an unlock date and leaves credentials with a legal representative.

Ethical Note: The platform is not a substitute for a legal will; official procedures should be followed.

Emergency Sharing

Securely delivers passwords or critical data to relatives if something happens to the individual.

Sample Flow: The user saves an emergency note, and stores the ID and password with a trusted person.

Ethical Note: Information should only be shared with trusted parties; responsibility lies with the user.

Threat / Exposure Scenario

Data you want revealed if you are harmed remains locked until the defined conditions are met.

Sample Flow: Evidence-like data is saved and instructions left with reliable intermediaries.

Ethical Note: The service does not support illegal or harmful activities; compliance with law is mandatory.

Valuable Deposit Notice

Allows elderly individuals to securely pass information about valuables or locations to trusted people.

Sample Flow: Information is saved, the ID and password to be shared with family members via a proxy.

Ethical Note: Consent of all parties and legal conformity must be ensured for shared information.
